Foundation of Wayne Enterprises

1965

Wayne Enterprises is founded by Dr. Thomas Wayne, laying the foundation for Gotham's most influential company.

Expansion into Technology

1978

Wayne Enterprises expands into the technology sector, leading to breakthroughs in computing and engineering.

Establishment of Wayne Foundation

1983

The Wayne Foundation is established to support community initiatives and philanthropic efforts across Gotham, including the now famous Wayne Soup Kitchen.

Bruce Wayne Becomes CEO

2000

Bruce Wayne takes over as CEO, steering the company towards a future-focused and innovative direction.

Launch of Wayne Aerospace Division

2010

The launch of the Wayne Aerospace Division marks Wayne Enterprises' entry into the aerospace industry, focusing on space exploration and defense technologies.

Commitment to Green Energy

2022

Wayne Enterprises announces its commitment to green energy, investing heavily in renewable energy sources and sustainable practices.
